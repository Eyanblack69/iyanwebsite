ICT

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) encompasses a broad spectrum of technologies and tools used to manage, process, communicate, and exchange information in today's digital world. From hardware components like computers, servers, and networking devices to software applications and internet-based platforms, ICT plays a pivotal role in facilitating the creation, storage, retrieval, transmission, and manipulation of data. ICT not only enables seamless communication and collaboration across diverse geographical locations but also drives innovation, automation, and efficiency across various industries and sectors. From businesses leveraging ICT for streamlined operations and enhanced customer experiences to educators integrating technology into teaching and learning processes, ICT permeates every aspect of modern society, shaping how we work, learn, communicate, and interact on a global scale.

GAS

General Academic Strand is what "GAS" usually stands for. A number of high schools, mostly in the Philippines, offer it as an academic track. Including courses from the social sciences, natural sciences, humanities, and mathematics, the General Academic Strand aims to give students a wide breadth of knowledge in these and other fields. The GAS track attracts students who may not have decided on a career path yet and have a wide range of interests. Students who choose the GAS route can study a variety of subjects and gain analytical, communication, and critical thinking abilities that are useful in a wide range of academic and professional settings. Offering a comprehensive education, it can ready pupils for immediate entry into the workforce following high school or for continued study in college.

TVL

In the Philippines, high school students who choose to pursue the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track are provided with a customized curriculum that focuses on building practical skills and competences in a variety of technical and vocational subjects. Students learn about a variety of topics through practical instruction and real-world experiences, including automotive technology, culinary arts, electronics, information technology, tourism, agribusiness, and beauty care. The goal of the TVL track is to provide students with marketable skills that will enable them to enter the workforce right away after graduation. Further education and training possibilities in technical and vocational schools are also provided by the track, allowing students to seek higher degrees of specialization and skill in their chosen sectors and eventually advancing their personal and professional development.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, or STEM, is an interdisciplinary approach to education and problem-solving that combines these four disciplines. Skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, innovation, and collaboration are emphasized in STEM education, which prepares students for a variety of careers in industries like computer science, engineering, healthcare, finance, and more.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences is what "HUMSS" stands for. It's a track or academic strand that some high schools, mostly in the Philippines, provide. The humanities (literature, philosophy, and history) and social sciences (sociology, anthropology, and political science) are the main areas of study for the HUMSS stream. The HUMSS program is often chosen by students who are curious about society, culture, human behavior, and societal challenges. Students pursuing this strand will be well-prepared for jobs in education, social work, law, media, public administration, and other fields requiring critical thinking, effective communication, and an awareness of human society and culture.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management, or ABM for short, is an academic track or strand that is offered in some high schools, especially in the Philippines. Students who want to pursue careers in business, finance, economics, management, marketing, and related fields usually choose the ABM track. The ABM strand equips students with knowledge and skills in areas like financial management, business ethics, entrepreneurship, strategic management, and business communication, preparing them for college programs and careers in the business world.
